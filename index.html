Krish Singal

Blog

Hello! I am a senior at Columbia University studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I am currently applying to PhD programs in Computer Science with focuses in sublinear algorithms for massive data and fine-grained complexity. My particular interests revolve around streaming/sketching algorithms, property testing, dynamic algorithms and data structures, and online algorithms in adversarial settings.

In summer 2023, I researched graph theory as part of the SMALL REU program. Before that, I researched bug-finding and its computational complexity. I have also interned as a software engineer at Apple and Amazon Web Services.

Outside of academics, I enjoy playing and writing music occasionally. Some of my other interests include science fiction novels, table tennis, and speed cubing.
