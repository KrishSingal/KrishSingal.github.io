Blog

Hello! I am a senior at Columbia University studying computer science and mathematics. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science and sublinear algorithm design. In summer 2023, I researched graph theory as part of the SMALL REU program. In the past, I have interned as a software engineer at Apple and AWS. In my free time, I enjoy playing table tennis, writing fiction, playing music, and speed cubing.

Feel free to reach out to me at krish.singal [at] columbia.edu. Take a look at some of my work below!
