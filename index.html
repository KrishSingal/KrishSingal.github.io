Miscellaneous

Hello! I am a senior at Columbia University. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science. Most recently, I've enjoyed thinking about sublinear algorithms.


In the past, I have worked at Apple and Amazon Web Services as a software intern. In summer 2023, I researched graph theory as part of the SMALL REU program at Williams College.


I enjoy teaching and mentorship. I am currently a mentor for the Columbia Undergraduate Learning Seminar in Theoretical CS. I have also served as a TA in the Columbia CS department for 3 semesters.
