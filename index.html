I am currently a PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania, where I am extremely grateful to be advised by Erik Waingarten and Sanjeev Khanna. Previously, I studied computer science and mathematics at Columbia University, where I was fortunate to have been mentored by Alexandr Andoni and Xi Chen. Broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of algorithm design and machine learning.
